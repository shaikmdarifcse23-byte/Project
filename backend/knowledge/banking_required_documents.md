# Required Banking Documents Knowledge Base

This document is used by FinAssist AI as retrieval context. Requirements vary by bank, country, product, customer risk profile, and regulatory rules. Users should confirm final requirements with their bank's official website, app, branch, or verified helpline.

## Savings Account Opening

Required documents commonly include:

- Completed account opening form
- Identity proof such as passport, voter ID, driving licence, Aadhaar, national ID, or other government-issued ID
- Address proof such as utility bill, bank statement, rent agreement, passport, Aadhaar, or official address document
- PAN, tax identification number, or equivalent tax declaration where applicable
- Recent passport-size photograph
- Mobile number and email address for verification
- Nominee details
- Initial deposit if required by the account type

Important notes:

- Do not share OTP, PIN, CVV, passwords, or full card numbers with anyone.
- Submit documents only through the official bank app, website, branch, or verified representative.

## KYC Update

Required documents commonly include:

- Signed KYC update form or customer information form
- Valid identity proof
- Current address proof
- PAN, tax ID, or equivalent declaration where applicable
- Recent photograph if requested
- Mobile and email verification
- Additional documents for high-risk, business, NRI, or non-resident profiles

KYC update may be required when documents expire, address changes, name changes, risk category changes, or periodic re-verification is due.

## Personal Loan Application

Required documents commonly include:

- Loan application form
- Identity proof
- Address proof
- PAN or tax ID
- Recent photographs
- Salary slips or income proof
- Bank statements, commonly for the last three to six months
- Employment proof, employee ID, or appointment letter for salaried customers
- Income tax returns, GST returns, business registration, or profit-and-loss statement for self-employed customers
- Credit bureau consent or credit report authorization

Eligibility usually depends on income, credit score, existing EMIs, employment stability, age, residence stability, and bank policy.

## Home Loan or Secured Loan

Required documents commonly include:

- Identity proof and address proof
- PAN or tax ID
- Income proof and bank statements
- Property title documents
- Sale agreement or allotment letter
- Approved building plan where applicable
- Encumbrance certificate or property search report where applicable
- Valuation, legal, and technical reports requested by the bank
- Insurance or collateral documents where applicable

## Debit Card, ATM Card, or Credit Card Replacement

Required documents commonly include:

- Card replacement request
- Identity proof
- Customer ID or masked account reference
- Card blocking complaint/reference number if the card was lost, stolen, or blocked
- Address confirmation for delivery

The user should immediately block a lost or stolen card through official bank channels.

## Fraud Report or Transaction Dispute

Required documents commonly include:

- Dispute or chargeback form
- Transaction date, amount, merchant name, and reference number
- Screenshots, SMS alerts, email alerts, or app notifications
- Card/account blocking reference number if applicable
- Bank complaint reference number
- Police complaint or cybercrime complaint acknowledgement if required
- Written statement describing what happened

Urgent action:

- Block the affected card, account, UPI, or wallet immediately.
- Change passwords and revoke suspicious device sessions.
- Contact the bank only through official numbers or channels.
- Never share OTP, PIN, CVV, UPI PIN, passwords, or remote screen access.

## Insurance Through Bank

Required documents commonly include:

- Application or proposal form
- Identity proof
- Address proof
- Age proof
- Income proof for some life insurance or high-value policies
- Nominee details
- Medical records or tests where required
- Existing policy details for renewal or portability

For claims, banks or insurers may ask for:

- Claim form
- Policy number
- Bank details
- Incident records, medical records, death certificate, or hospital documents depending on claim type
- Nominee or legal heir documents where applicable
